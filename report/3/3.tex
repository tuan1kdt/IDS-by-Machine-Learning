\section{Proposed System: An ML-Based IDS Pipeline}
\label{sec:models}

Section~\ref{sec:background} established the conceptual ingredients required for the task. We now consolidate them into a concrete system: a flow-level multiclass classifier trained on CIC-IDS2017 and deployed behind a streaming inference engine. Subsection~\ref{sec:problem-statement} formalises the learning problem and its operational constraints; Subsection~\ref{sec:arch-overview} describes the end-to-end pipeline; Subsections~\ref{sec:feat-eng} and~\ref{sec:model-design} detail feature engineering and model design; Subsection~\ref{sec:training} specifies the training procedure including pseudocode; finally, Subsection~\ref{sec:inference} describes the real-time inference engine that consumes the trained models in production.
