\section{Background and Foundations}
\label{sec:background}
%=======================================================================

This section reviews the domain knowledge and technical foundations required to follow the rest of the paper. We begin with a primer on network traffic and the taxonomy of network-based attacks (Section~\ref{sec:bg-network}), then describe the CIC-IDS2017 dataset and the meaning of its flow-level features (Section~\ref{sec:bg-dataset}). Section~\ref{sec:bg-preproc} summarizes the data-preprocessing techniques---including class-imbalance handling and normalization---that we later apply to prepare the dataset for training. Finally, Section~\ref{sec:bg-ml} outlines the machine learning formalism relevant to our multiclass classification problem and the evaluation metrics used throughout the experiments.
