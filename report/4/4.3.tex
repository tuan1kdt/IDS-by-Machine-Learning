Key observations:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{High-volume attacks} (DDoS, DoS Hulk, PortScan, Brute Force FTP/SSH) are detected with F1 scores above 98\% by all models, as there are sufficient training examples.
    \item \textbf{Rare attacks} (Heartbleed, SQL Injection, Infiltration) show notably lower F1 scores (65--88\%), reflecting the challenge of class imbalance even with SMOTE.
    \item \textbf{XGBoost consistently outperforms} both Random Forest and DNN across all attack types, with the largest margins on rare attack categories.
\end{itemize}
